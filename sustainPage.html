The challenge

The Office of Sustainability is a hub for sustainability and sustainable development with a focus on applied research, experiential learning, operational management, and public engagement on campus and in the greater Charleston community. My task as a intern was to create a mobile application that brought the College of Charelsotn expirence to the hands of students.

The solution

For the past year, I have been the sole developer and an active contributor to the project. I have crafted the application from the ground up, first in Swift, and currently in React-Native for multiplatform capabilities. The project itself has been delayed by larger institutional inertia, but launch is scheduled to be Fall 2019.
